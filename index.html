The Solution

The DMT System — Simple as a Game

Five steps. Any chart. Any market. Any timeframe. This is the complete DMT framework from open to close.

01
📐
Channel
Identify a clear channel on any timeframe. Tight or broad. Uptrend or downtrend.
02
💥
Break
Wait for the candle to close decisively beyond the channel boundary. Breakout or fakeout — both valid.
03
💧
Liquidity
Count the sweeps: LQ1 → LQ2 (more stretched) → LQ3. LQ3 is your entry zone. Never early.
04
🎯
Entry
Enter at LQ3 with a candle signal. Stop = 1–2 candles. Calculate 0.25% lot size. Always.
05
✅
Exit
Hold to old LQ targets. Take parcels. Move SL to break even. Exit at 3 pushes or 1:3 ratio minimum.
🐘
Elephant Trade — Tight Channel
Trade with the trend in a tight directional channel. Entry: 20MA/9MA above price OR LQ present OR strong green bar. Hold while channel stays tight.
♟
Bishop + Queen — Broad Channel Break
Wait for channel break + liquidity taken in the Initial BO Zone. Double Monkey confirmation (3MJ + DLS both present) required before entry.
🔄
Reversal Trade — Any Channel
Double Bottom (BUY ✓ preferred) or Double Top forms. Returns to BO Start. LQ of DB/DT → Wedge. Check timing. Enter above Bull Bar. Very tight stop.
⚡
Up Ride Scalp — Green Channel Only
Big TF (4HR/Daily) in tight green channel UP → drop to 1M → micro LQ → micro double bottom → enter. Never move SL in scalp. Take. Close. Leave.
